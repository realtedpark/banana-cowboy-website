About the Team

Banana Cowboy started as a college project, built over a semester with a group of 7 students from UT Austin. Drawing inspiration from Super Mario Galaxy and A Hat In Time, Banana Cowboy released to Steam with a "Very Positive" rating.

Since then, the team decided to persue a fully polished remake with improvements to models, gameplay and story. The team has aquired new members, industry professionals with the experience to bring the team's goals to fruition.
