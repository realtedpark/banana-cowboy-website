Banana Cowboy: The Wild Bunch

Banana Cowboy wanted a simple life serving as sheirf on the isolated planet of Durian Gap with his deputy Prickly Pete. However, his past wouldn't leave him be. Forced out of hiding by the famed bounty hunter The Quiet, Banana Cowboy must travel the Galaxy to prove his innocence of a murder he didn't commit. Along the way, he must face down his old friends from The Pepper Gang.
